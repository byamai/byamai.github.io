Research

Watching surgery, frame by frame.

Researcher · UHN — Toronto General

I worked on detection models for operating-room videos — identifying which instrument is in frame, whether it sits in a safe or unsafe zone, and when dissection begins.

Once the team's models were trained, I went back through the original footage frame by frame to see where the predictions held up.

1 · Detect. The model finds the instrument.
2 · Map. The frame's safe and unsafe zones.
3 · Check. Instrument fully inside the safe zone — this frame passes.
